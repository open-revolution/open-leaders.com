Openness is transforming our societies and economies. On this course you get to explore the ideas, possibilities and communities in this rapidly growing space.

Examine the detailed reasoning and case studies for open transformation – whilst developing and practicing the key organising, speaking, convening and leadership skills needed to make a real difference in the world and drive openness forward.
